Learning Reflection

The moments I was most proud of would be when I completed the assignments. That feeling after submitting something in, and looking back at what I created makes me surprised that I even did that in the first place.

A tough situation or barrier for me would be asking for help when I am lost or stuck on an error. There were many moments I was having battles with myself during times of help and then asking for help only for the answer to be super simple.

I am constantly thinking between if I would or wouldn't do it over again. If I was to do something again it would probably the fictional character assignment because I would probably want to make it look even better than it does now with the knowledge I have now.

What actions/behaviours should you start doing that would help you in future courses? I don't know what actions/behaviors I would start doing that would help. I think what I am doing now is the best I can do.
