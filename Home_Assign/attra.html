There are many things to do in Lethbridge. Shop at the Park Place Mall with over 100 stores or explore downtown and find many local businesses. Learn more about Lethbridge's history at the Galt Museum. Take a drive down to the river bottom and get up close to the Old Man River or High-Level Bridge as you take in nature. Follow up with a visit to Fort Whoop-Up and Even Helen Schuler Nature Center. There are also many places to go around the outside Lethbridge area. Take a visit to Waterton National Park or go to Coaldale and See Alberta's Birds of Prey Centre.
