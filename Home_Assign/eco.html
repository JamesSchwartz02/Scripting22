Since Lethbridge is built on prairie land, most of Lethbridge's economy is historically from agriculture. An abundance of coal deposits meant Lethbridge was home to a mine for a considerable part of time in its early history until 1957.

Lethbridge has both a professional hockey and baseball team with the Western Hockey League Hurricanes playing in the Enmax Centre. The Lethbridge Bulls play at the Spitz Stadium through the summer months. There are also teams for the University of Lethbridge and Lethbridge College. The Lethbridge Pronghorns represents the university, and the college team is the Lethbridge Kodiaks.
