Lethbridge is a small city surrounded by history. You can see plenty of old structures mixed with modern buildings creating a unique feel of a town that's been here for a long time. Many locals enjoy taking walks in parks or riding their bikes on bike paths around Lethbridge. There are 130 parks and 112 bike paths for you to enjoy. Locals also enjoy road trips to other cities or parks around southern Alberta. Tourists will have plenty of places and things to do within the Lethbridge area.
