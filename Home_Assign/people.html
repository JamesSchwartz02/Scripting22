Lethbridge has a diverse group of people living in the city. According to the Census conducted by Statistics Canada in 2021, the total population at the time was reported to be 123,847. Lethbridge is a friendly and welcoming city with locals who will stop to help with anything you need.

Lethbridge and area have a history with the Blackfoot confederacy and Indigenous people who make this land their home. Lethbridge sits on the Blackfoot people's grounds and takes pride in representing their culture. Japanese culture is also represented in the city. You can visit the Buddhist temple and learn more about Japanese culture at the Nikka Yuko Japanese Gardens.
