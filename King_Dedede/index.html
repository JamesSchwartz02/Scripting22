About

King Dedede is the archrival of Kirby. He lives in Dream Land, a country that is found on Planet Popstar and is a peaceful country with plenty of resources. King Dedede is only "king" because he gave himself that title. He wields a giant hammer that holds a rocket engine inside and loves to eat food.

Characteristics

  • Lazy: King Dedede is known to show little interest in ruling Dream Land and would rather sit in his castle and eat food.
  • Greedy: Since he loves food, he will actively command his minions to steal food around the country and eat it all for himself.
  • Selfish: King Dedede will alawys try to prove to himself that he is better than Kirby. King Dedede has trained countless days to fly, and inhale like Kirby.
  • Caring: There has been moments where King Dedede has shown to have a heart of gold towards others, and the kingdom.

Friends

  • Kirby: The main character of the Kirby series and is King Dedede's archrival and friend. Kirby has been undefeated against King Dedede's schemes. Kirby has throughout the series saved Planet Popstar multiple times from fierce entities.
  • Meta Knight: A skilled warrior that has mystrious motives. Meta Knight has sided with King Dedede to fight against Kirby but he has also sided with Kirby to fight whatever threatens Dream Land. Meta Knight commands a airship called Battleship Halberd.
  • Bandana Waddle Dee: A waddle dee who is a very loyal follower to King Dedede, but will disobey him to help his friend Kirby.
  • Escargoon: A character from the anime Kirby: Right Back at Ya! He is King Dedede's right-hand man for everything King Dedede needs and wants.
